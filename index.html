About

We opened our little artisanal shop that made traditionally crafted ice cream on the south shore of Québec City. In those days, the shop specialized primarily in making homemade ice cream for small event. Gradually, as time passed, our artisans started to develop more and more products. Having started out in home kitchen, then small shop, we have now a recognized ice cream shop.


Yumyum homemade and secret recipe catapulted the company’s reputation as one of summer’s most eagerly awaited treats. People from all over the region visited the shop to enjoy it.


Yumyum continued to reinvent the traditional concept of homemade ice cream shop. We developed a magical new concept of stores that offered a spectacular flavours.
